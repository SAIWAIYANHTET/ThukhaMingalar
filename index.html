About Us

Bringing You the Best of Myanmar,

Seamlessly and Affordably

We "Thukha Mingalar Chanmyae Travel & Tours" is an experienced internal tourism company. The head office is based in Yangon and the representative offices are set up in all related tourism zones. Our main services include inbound Myanmar packages: Classic & Culture Tours, Luxury & Leisure, Adventure & Trekking, Honeymoon, Biking, Diving, Cruises, Eco & Countryside, Tailor-made and Individual Tours, as well as outbound tours to other Asian and European countries.
