Classis and Culture Tour

We "Thukha Mingalar Chanmyay Travel & Tours" is an experienced internal tourism company. The head office is based in Yangon and the representative offices are set up in all related tourism zone. Our main tourism services are Inbound Myanmar Package tour for Classic and Culture Tour, Luxury and Leisure Tour, Adventure and Trekking Tour, Honeymoon Tour, Biking, Diving, Cruise, Eco and County side Tour, Tailor made Tour, & individual Tour for around Myanmar and Outbound Package Tour for other Asia and Europe Countries.

12 Days Best of Myanmar Tour

  1. The glorious Shwedagon pagoda, heart of Yangon.
  2. Bagan Kingdom and its amazing ancient and architectural sites and breathtaking sunset.
  3. The royal capital Mandalay with the panoramic view of Mandalay View.
  4. Feel the nature and lifestyle of floating village, Inle Lake.
  5. Enjoy the scenic view of the countryside.
  6. Discover the ancient cities around Myanmar.
